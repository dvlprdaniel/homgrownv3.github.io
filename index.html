About

We, as a locally owned, small community OLCC licensed dispensary, provide safe access to cannabis and cannabis products for recreational users and OMMP medical patients alike. HomeGrown Remedies provides a judgement free environment for anyone with an interest in cannabis. We strive to interact consciously and responsibly with everyone in our community. Our goal is to change the common misperception and stereotype by raising the bar with our standards of quality, professionalism, and service.
